Movin' Right Along:
A Timeline of Jim Henson's Career

  • 1936

     Born on September 24, 1936 in Greenville, Mississippi

  • 1954

     Goes to the University of Maryland

  • 1955

     "Sam and Friends" begins airing live on WRCTV out of Washington, D.C.

  • 1958

     "Sam and Friends" wins a local Emmy award

  • 1959

     Marries Jane Nebel

  • 1960

     The Muppets have their premier appearanc on "The Today Show"

  • 1966

     The Muppets are used for several product commercials
    and licensed products of Kermit and Rowlf are released.

  • 1969

     "Sesame Street" pilot and first show are taped, creating Big Bird, Bert, Ernie and others

  • 1976

     First season of "The Muppet Show"

  • 1978

     "The Muppet Movie" premieres

  • 1981

     "The Great Muppet Caper" movie premiers

  • 1982

     "The Dark Crystal" movie premieres

  • 1983

     "Fraggle Rock" tv show debuts on HBO

  • 1984

     "The Muppets Take Manhattan" movie premieres
    "Jim Henson's Muppet Babies" animated television series created

  • 1985

     "The Labyrinth" with George Lucas and David Bowie premieres
    30 year anniversary of The Muppets

  • 1990

     Produces "Muppet*Vision 3-D" to be shown in Hollywood Studios at Walt Disney World
    Jim Henson dies on May 16 in New York City
    "The Muppet Celebrate Jim Henson" TV special airs

  • 1991

     Honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

  • 1992

     The Jim Henson Legacy is established by Jane Henson
