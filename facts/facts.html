A Man and his Muppets

Kermit the Frog

First created in 1955 for "Sam and Friends," Jim Henson described him as "literally my right hand."
Henson performed as Kermit up until his death in 1990 in shows and movies including
Sesame Street, The Muppet Show, The Muppet Movie, The Great Muppet Caper, and The Muppets Take Manhattan.
He performed several hit songs including "Bein' Green" and "Rainbow Connection," which reached No. 25 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1979.
